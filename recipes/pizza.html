Pizza

Description

This is a pizza, and the recipe we’re going to use is the absolute BEST! If you’re interested in making your very own pizza from scratch, then this is the recipe you’re going to want to use!

This is an authentic recipe that comes directly from Italy. My family has used this very same recipe for the past 88 years. Since this pizza means so much to the people in my family, I hope you’re able to bring the same joy to your family as well when making this authentic Italian pizza!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Fresh Tomato Paste
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Start with the dough and make a circle about 12in. in diameter
  2. Make a nice crust around the edge and fill the center with your tomato paste
  3. Add light cheese to the top of your pizza
  4. Bake for approximately 13 minutes at 400 degrees
  5. Top with fresh basil
  6. Enjoy!
