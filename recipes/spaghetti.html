Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a perfect blend of tomato sauce, meat, and noodles.

To start, we’re going to add meat to medium high heat. Once we get our meat to a nice brown color, we can add in our vegetables and tomato sauce. Cook on high heat for 10 minutes, and then cook the spaghetti for an additional 50 minutes on low heat. When your meat sauce has about 30 minutes left, you can add your noodles to boiling water and cook to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1lb. ground meat
  • Half pound noodles
  • One can of tomato sauce
  • Your desired vegetables

Steps

  1. Mince vegetables
  2. Add ground meat to medium high heat
  3. Add vegetables to ground meat once it is brown
  4. Add tomato sauce
  5. Cook on high heat for 10 minutes
  6. Reduce heat to low and cook 50 more minutes
  7. Cook noodles and add sauce
  8. Bon appetite!
